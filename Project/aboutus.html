Lunar Society seeks to not only sell high quality and trendy fashion, but to sell an experience that leaves our target audience
with appreciation for creativity.

We are an Atlanta based clothing brand that focuses on creativity, individuality and non-traditional ways of expression.
Founded by Rem Valentine, Lunar Society aims to make clothing that pushes boundaries and shows young people different ways
of expressing their personalities through fashion.
